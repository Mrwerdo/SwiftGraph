UniqueElementsGraph

open class UniqueElementsGraph<V> : UnweightedGraph<V> where V : Equatable

A subclass of UnweightedGraph that ensures there are no pairs of equal vertices and no repeated edges.

    Creates a new UniqueVerticesGraph that is the union of two UniqueVerticesGraphs. O(n^2)

    This operation is commutative in the sense that g1 ∪ g2 has the same vertices and edges than g2 ∪ g1. However, the indices of the vertices are not guaranteed to be the same.
